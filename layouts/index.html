CAPCI PROFILE

The Argentina Association of Producers of Integrated Cherries (CAPCI) is an organization consisting on the leading producers and exporters of cherries in the country, representing 70% of exports from Argentina

The Association has the following objectives:

  • Promote Argentine cherries to the world
  • Open new export markets
  • Address matters related to phytosanitary crops
  • Facilitate the development and access to relevant information and innovation by supporting cherries industries

PRODUCT PROFILE

Argentine cherries are produced in arid and cold environments, such as Patagonia, of recognized health, free of diseases such as Ceratitis capitata, Anastrepha and bactrocera. It is offered between November and March, producing the most southern fruit in the world, cultivated in the ancient province of Santa Cruz - Patagonia. The offer of early fruit can be sent by air directly from Patagonia.

Argentine cherries are distinguished by their health standard, high quality, excellent size, color and sweetness.
